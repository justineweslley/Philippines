Boracay Beach

Aklan, Western Visayas, Philippines

Boracay is a small island in the province of Aklan, Western Visayas, best known for its powdery white-sand beaches, clear turquoise waters, and vibrant island nightlife. Its most famous coastline, White Beach, has consistently been ranked among the best beaches in the world.

After a major island rehabilitation in 2018, Boracay now follows stricter environmental guidelines, preserving its natural beauty while still offering world-class resorts, water activities, and dining experiences.

  • Location: Aklan, Western Visayas, Philippines
  • Known For: White-sand beaches, crystal-clear water, island nightlife
  • Main Beach: White Beach (4 km long)
  • Best Time to Visit: November–May (dry season)
